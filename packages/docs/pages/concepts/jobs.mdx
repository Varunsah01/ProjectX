# Jobs

A **Job** is a field visit or task assigned to a technician. Jobs can be planned (scheduled from a contract) or reactive (created from a complaint or ad hoc).

## Job Types

| Type | When Used |
|---|---|
| **Planned Maintenance** | Scheduled service visits from an AMC contract |
| **Reactive** | Unplanned visits in response to a complaint or breakdown |
| **Installation** | New equipment setup |
| **Ad Hoc** | One-off visits not tied to a contract |

## Job Lifecycle

```
Scheduled → Assigned → In Progress → Completed
                                   ↘ Cancelled
```

| Status | Description |
|---|---|
| Scheduled | Job created, not yet assigned to a technician |
| Assigned | Technician assigned, not yet started |
| In Progress | Technician has checked in on-site |
| Completed | Work done, report submitted |
| Cancelled | Job will not be executed |

## Creating a Job

**From a contract:**
1. Open the contract → click **Schedule Job**
2. Select date, time, and technician
3. Save — the technician receives a push notification

**From a complaint:**
1. Open the complaint → click **Create Job**
2. The job is automatically linked to the complaint

**Ad hoc:**
1. Sidebar → **Jobs → New Job**
2. Select customer, optionally link assets/contract

## The Mobile Flow

Technicians use the Recuring mobile app to execute jobs:

1. **Receive notification** — "New job assigned: AC servicing at Acme Corp"
2. **Check in** — Tap **Start Job**; GPS coordinates recorded
3. **Complete work** — Update checklist items
4. **Upload photo proof** — Take photos of the equipment before/after
5. **Collect signature** — Customer signs on-screen to confirm completion
6. **Submit report** — Job status changes to **Completed**

See [Mobile Field Ops →](/how-tos/mobile-field-ops) for the full technician walkthrough.

## Job Reports

After a job is completed, a **service report** is available for download. It includes:
- Technician name and check-in/check-out times
- Work performed (checklist)
- Photos uploaded
- Customer signature
- Asset details

Reports can be emailed directly to the customer from the job detail page.
