# Concepts

This section explains the core data model behind Recuring and how the key entities relate to each other.

## Data Model at a Glance

```
Organisation
└── Customer
    ├── Asset (equipment under service)
    ├── Contract (AMC / recurring agreement)
    │   └── Invoice (auto-generated per billing cycle)
    ├── Complaint (support ticket)
    └── Job (field visit, linked to contract or complaint)
        └── Technician (assigned staff member)
```

## Core Entities

| Entity | What it represents |
|---|---|
| [Customer](/concepts/customers) | A company or individual you provide services to |
| [Asset](/concepts/assets) | Equipment or property the customer owns that you maintain |
| [Contract](/concepts/contracts) | A recurring service agreement — defines billing schedule and scope |
| [Job](/concepts/jobs) | A field visit or task assigned to a technician |
| [Complaint](/concepts/complaints) | A customer-raised issue or service request |

## How They Connect

- A **Contract** belongs to a **Customer** and may be linked to one or more **Assets**.
- **Invoices** are auto-generated from a **Contract** based on its billing schedule.
- **Jobs** are created from **Contracts** (planned maintenance) or **Complaints** (reactive visits).
- A **Technician** is assigned to a **Job** and executes it via the mobile app.
