# Complaints & Tickets

A **Complaint** (also called a ticket) is a customer-raised issue or service request. Complaints track the full lifecycle from receipt to resolution and can trigger reactive jobs.

## Complaint Fields

| Field | Description |
|---|---|
| Customer | Which customer raised the complaint |
| Asset | Which equipment the complaint is about (optional) |
| Title | Short summary of the issue |
| Description | Full details |
| Priority | Low / Medium / High / Critical |
| Status | Open / In Progress / Resolved / Closed |
| Assigned To | Technician or support agent handling it |
| SLA Due | Deadline for resolution based on priority |

## Raising a Complaint

**By admin/staff:**
1. Sidebar → **Complaints → New Complaint**
2. Select customer, optionally link to an asset
3. Set priority and assign to a team member

**By customer (via portal):**
1. Customer logs into the portal
2. Goes to **Support → Raise a Complaint**
3. Fills in title, description, and optionally uploads a photo
4. Complaint appears in your dashboard immediately

## Complaint Statuses

| Status | Description |
|---|---|
| Open | Received, not yet assigned or acknowledged |
| In Progress | Being worked on |
| Resolved | Fix applied; awaiting customer confirmation |
| Closed | Confirmed resolved by customer or auto-closed |

## Priority and SLA

| Priority | Target Resolution Time |
|---|---|
| Low | 5 business days |
| Medium | 2 business days |
| High | 1 business day |
| Critical | 4 hours |

SLA timers start when the complaint is created and pause outside business hours.

> **Note:** SLA configuration (business hours, escalation rules) is currently fixed at these defaults. Custom SLA tiers are on the roadmap.

## Creating a Job from a Complaint

When a complaint requires a site visit:

1. Open the complaint
2. Click **Create Job**
3. The job is linked to the complaint; the complaint status moves to **In Progress**

Once the job is completed, the complaint status updates to **Resolved** automatically.

## Complaint History

Every complaint has a full activity log showing:
- When it was created and by whom (or "Customer Portal")
- Status changes with timestamps
- Comments from staff
- Linked jobs
- Attachments and photos
