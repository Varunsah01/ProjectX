# Getting Started

This section walks you through setting up Recuring from scratch — from creating your account to sending your first invoice.

## In This Section

1. **[Sign Up & Setup](/getting-started/signup)** — Create your account, configure organisation details, add GSTIN/PAN, upload your logo, and connect your bank account.

2. **[Your First Customer](/getting-started/first-customer)** — Add a customer record with billing address, GST number, and contact details.

3. **[Your First Invoice](/getting-started/first-invoice)** — Create a GST-compliant invoice and send it via email or WhatsApp with a Razorpay payment link.

## Before You Begin

Make sure you have:
- Your **GSTIN** (if GST-registered) and **PAN number**
- Your **company logo** (PNG or JPG, recommended 512×512 px)
- **Razorpay API keys** if you want online payment collection (you can add these later)
- A list of your existing customers and contracts (for bulk import later)
