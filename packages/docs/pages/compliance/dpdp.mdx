# DPDP Compliance

Recuring complies with the **Digital Personal Data Protection Act, 2023** (DPDP Act). This page explains your rights as a Data Principal and how to exercise them.

## Who This Applies To

The DPDP Act applies to:
- **Recuring customers** (organisations that use Recuring) — as Data Principals for their own account and employee data
- **End customers** (individuals whose data you store in Recuring) — as Data Principals for their personal data

## Your Rights Under DPDP

### Right to Access
You have the right to know what personal data we hold about you and how it is being processed.

**How to exercise:** Email [grievance@recuring.in](mailto:grievance@recuring.in) with the subject "Data Access Request — [your email]". We will respond within **30 days**.

### Right to Correction
You have the right to correct inaccurate or incomplete personal data.

**How to exercise:**
- Org account holders: Update directly in **Settings → Organisation** or **Settings → Profile**.
- For data you cannot update yourself: email [grievance@recuring.in](mailto:grievance@recuring.in).

### Right to Erasure
You have the right to request deletion of your personal data.

**Limitations:** Data required for legal or regulatory compliance (e.g., GST records, 7-year financial data retention) cannot be deleted before the legally mandated period.

**How to exercise:** Email [grievance@recuring.in](mailto:grievance@recuring.in) with the subject "Data Erasure Request — [your email]". We will confirm within **30 days** and complete deletion within **60 days** unless a legal hold applies.

### Right to Grievance Redressal
You have the right to raise a complaint about how your data is processed.

**How to exercise:** See [Grievance Officer →](/compliance/grievance).

### Right of Nomination
You may nominate another individual to exercise your DPDP rights on your behalf in the event of your death or incapacity.

**How to exercise:** Email [grievance@recuring.in](mailto:grievance@recuring.in) with a signed nomination form (template available on request).

## Data Principal Consent

Recuring processes personal data under the following lawful bases:
- **Contract**: Necessary to provide the service you subscribed to
- **Consent**: For marketing communications (you may withdraw at any time via the unsubscribe link in emails)
- **Legal obligation**: For tax records retained as required by law

## Data Breach Notification

In the event of a personal data breach:
- We will notify the **Data Protection Board of India** within **72 hours** of becoming aware
- We will notify affected Data Principals **without undue delay** where the breach is likely to result in high risk
