# Grievance Officer

Under the **Digital Personal Data Protection Act, 2023** and **IT Act, 2000 (Intermediary Guidelines)**, Recuring has appointed a Grievance Officer to handle data protection and platform grievances.

## Contact Details

**Email:** [grievance@recuring.in](mailto:grievance@recuring.in)

**Business Hours:** Monday to Friday, 10:00 AM – 6:00 PM IST (excluding public holidays)

## How to Raise a Grievance

1. Email [grievance@recuring.in](mailto:grievance@recuring.in) with:
   - Your full name
   - Your email address (registered with Recuring or the email where your data is held)
   - Subject line clearly indicating the type of request:
     - `Data Access Request`
     - `Data Correction Request`
     - `Data Erasure Request`
     - `Grievance — [brief description]`
   - Description of the issue or request

2. You will receive an **acknowledgement within 48 hours** (business days).

3. We will **resolve the grievance within 30 days** of receipt. Complex cases involving legal analysis may take up to 60 days; we will notify you if this applies.

## Escalation

If you are unsatisfied with our response, you may escalate to the **Data Protection Board of India** once it is established and operational under the DPDP Act.

For court proceedings, disputes are subject to the jurisdiction of courts in **Bengaluru, Karnataka, India**.
