# Privacy Policy

*This is a summary. The full legally binding Privacy Policy is available at [recuring.in/privacy](https://recuring.in/privacy).*

## What We Collect

| Data Type | Examples | Purpose |
|---|---|---|
| Account data | Name, email, phone, password hash | Authentication and communication |
| Organisation data | Company name, GSTIN, address, logo | Invoice generation |
| Customer data | Your customers' names, emails, addresses | Providing the service to you |
| Transaction data | Invoice amounts, payment status | Financial records |
| Usage data | Page views, feature usage (anonymised) | Product improvement |
| Device data | IP address, browser/OS (mobile app) | Security and fraud prevention |

## What We Do Not Collect

- We do not sell your data to third parties.
- We do not use your customers' data for advertising.
- We do not store payment card numbers (Razorpay handles card data under PCI-DSS).

## Data Retention

| Data Type | Retention Period |
|---|---|
| Account and transaction data | 7 years (as required by Indian tax law) |
| Usage analytics | 2 years |
| Support tickets | 3 years |
| Deleted account data | Purged within 30 days of deletion request |

## Third-Party Processors

| Processor | Purpose | Location |
|---|---|---|
| Neon (AWS ap-south-1) | Database hosting | India |
| AWS S3 (ap-south-1) | File and image storage | India |
| Razorpay | Payment processing | India |
| Resend | Transactional email | India (data processed in EU under SCCs) |
| Sentry | Error monitoring | EU (no PII in error reports) |

## Your Rights

Under the **Digital Personal Data Protection Act 2023**, you have the right to:
- Access your personal data
- Correct inaccurate data
- Erase your data (subject to legal retention requirements)
- Nominate a person to exercise rights on your behalf

To exercise these rights, see [DPDP Compliance →](/compliance/dpdp).
