# Welcome to Recuring

Recuring is a **recurring service management platform** built for Indian service businesses — AMC providers, facility managers, equipment maintenance companies, and more. It replaces spreadsheets and WhatsApp threads with a single system that handles contracts, invoices, field jobs, and compliance.

## What Recuring Does

| Capability | Description |
|---|---|
| **Contracts & AMC** | Create recurring service contracts with flexible billing schedules (monthly, quarterly, annual). Auto-renew and auto-invoice at the right time. |
| **Customer Portal** | Give customers a branded self-service portal to view invoices, raise complaints, and download reports. |
| **Field Operations** | Assign jobs to technicians via the mobile app. Technicians update status, upload photo proof, and collect digital signatures on-site. |
| **Invoicing & GST** | Generate GST-compliant invoices (CGST/SGST/IGST) with Razorpay payment links embedded. |
| **Complaints & Tickets** | Log, assign, and resolve customer complaints with SLA tracking. |
| **Reports & Exports** | Revenue, collection, and operational reports. Tally/Zoho CSV export for accounting. |
