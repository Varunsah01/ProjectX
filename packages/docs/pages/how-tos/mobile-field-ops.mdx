# Mobile Field Ops

The Recuring mobile app is used by technicians to manage their assigned jobs in the field — from receiving the job notification to uploading completion proof.

## Installing the App

The app is available for:
- **Android**: [Google Play Store](#) *(search "Recuring Field")*
- **iOS**: [Apple App Store](#) *(search "Recuring Field")*

## Technician Account Setup

1. As an admin, go to **Technicians → Add Technician** and enter the technician's name, email, and phone.
2. The technician receives an email with a link to set their password.
3. They log in to the mobile app with their email and password.

## The Job Workflow

### 1. Receiving a Job

When a job is assigned to the technician, they receive a **push notification**:
> "New job: AC Servicing at Acme Corp — tomorrow, 10:00 AM"

The job appears on the **Today's Jobs** screen of the app.

### 2. Starting a Job

When the technician arrives on-site:
1. Open the job in the app.
2. Tap **Start Job**.
3. GPS coordinates are recorded as the check-in location.

The job status changes to **In Progress** and is visible in real-time on the admin dashboard.

### 3. Recording Work

The technician completes the checklist items defined for the job type:
- Each item can be marked **Done**, **Not Applicable**, or **Requires Follow-Up**
- Technicians can add notes per checklist item

### 4. Uploading Photo Proof

1. Tap **Add Photos**.
2. Take or select up to 10 photos (before/after equipment photos, work evidence).
3. Photos are uploaded to secure cloud storage and attached to the job report.

### 5. Customer Signature

1. Tap **Get Signature**.
2. Hand the phone to the customer.
3. The customer draws their signature on the screen and taps **Confirm**.
4. The signature is embedded in the job report.

### 6. Completing the Job

1. Tap **Complete Job**.
2. A summary is shown — checklist items, photos count, signature status.
3. Confirm → Job status changes to **Completed**.
4. A service report PDF is automatically generated and emailed to the customer.

## Offline Mode

The mobile app works offline for essential functions:
- Viewing assigned jobs
- Completing checklists
- Adding notes

Photos and signatures sync when connectivity is restored. The job cannot be marked **Completed** until photos and signature are uploaded.

## Admin Visibility

From the admin dashboard (**Jobs** view), admins can see:
- Real-time job status (Assigned / In Progress / Completed)
- Check-in GPS location on a map
- Photo thumbnails and signature as soon as they sync

## Technician Performance Reports

Go to **Reports → Technician Performance** to see:
- Jobs completed per technician per period
- Average job duration
- On-time completion rate (vs scheduled time)
- Customer complaint rate linked to each technician
